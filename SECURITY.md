# Security Policy

We take the security of Vaultlight seriously and appreciate your help in keeping the community safe.

## Supported Versions

We currently support the latest release on the `main` branch. Please verify that the issue exists on the latest commit before submitting a report.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please send security reports to `security@vaultlight.app` with the following details:

- A description of the vulnerability and the potential impact.
- Steps to reproduce the issue (proof of concept code, screenshots, or logs are helpful).
- Any known mitigations or workarounds.

We aim to acknowledge incoming reports within **3 business days** and provide a follow-up status update within **10 business days**. We may request additional information to reproduce and triage the issue.

## Disclosure Guidelines

- Do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we confirm a fix or provide guidance.
- Avoid accessing, modifying, or deleting data that does not belong to you.
- Once a fix is released, we will publicly credit you (with permission) in the release notes.

Thank you for helping us make Vaultlight safer for everyone.
